WHO WE ARE

Pitiyas Gemstones is a premier gemstone provider based in Ethiopia, a land renowned for its rich mineral heritage. We specialize in sourcing and delivering the finest gemstones found across the country, from the vibrant opals of Wollo Province to the deep emeralds and sapphires hidden within Ethiopia’s rugged landscapes.

Founded with a passion for showcasing Ethiopia’s natural treasures, our mission is to connect the world with the beauty and diversity of our homeland’s gemstones. Each stone we offer is a testament to the craftsmanship and sustainability we uphold, ensuring that every piece tells a story of origin and excellence.

At Pitiyas Gemstones, we are more than a company—we are a bridge between Ethiopia’s ancient geological wonders and modern luxury, committed to quality, authenticity, and the celebration of our unique heritage.
